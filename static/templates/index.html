Drug shortages have negative impacts on consumers, healthcare providers and pharmaceutical companies. The average drug shortage affects 500,000 consumers and results in a 16.6% increase of the price of drugs in shortage.
Our drug shortage prediction product enables healthcare professionals such as hospital operations managers and group purchasing organizations to manage drug shortages to reduce patient health impacts.
